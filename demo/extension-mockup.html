Marine biology lab: reef health indicators

Due Tomorrow by 2:15pm Points 20 Submitting Text entry File Types pdf, docx

Collect sample observations, compare reef health markers, and write a short interpretation of what the data suggests.

Your response should explain what patterns you notice, what the data may mean for reef health, and what further monitoring could help a local team learn next.

What to include

  • Review the field notes and lab sheet
  • Compare indicators across sample sites
  • Draft a short evidence-based explanation

Teacher note

Practice observation, data interpretation, and communicating what the data means for real places and communities.

Attached materials

  • Lab sheet: reef health markers
  • Field notes sample set
  • Quick guide: interpreting environmental indicators
